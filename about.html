About

Welcome to Clash of Clans Calculator! This site was made as a tool to help hardcore players learns the most they can about their troops, defenses, and resources. Using all the statistics available, the calculator can help you achieve your most strategically powerful army. It might also help highlight weaknesses you did not see. Either way, it is fun to play around with so give it a try and tell us some feedback on the contact page. We know this is always room for improvement. Thank you for checking out this site!!

The Clash of Clans Calculator breaks down your army into three elements. Troop, Defense, and Resources. From here, you are able to see statistics regarding their current level and upgrade potential stats as well.
